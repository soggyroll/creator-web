# Soggy Roll — What It Is, Why It Exists

## The short version

You want to run an AI generation workflow. You don't want to rent a GPU, configure a server, babysit a process, or pay a monthly subscription for a tool you'll use twice this week.

So don't. Just submit your job. We'll run it. You get the output.

---

## What you can do here

Bring a ComfyUI workflow — image generation, video, upscaling, whatever you've built — and run it without owning any hardware.

Upload your workflow, upload your files, hit go.

---

## How it works (the only part that matters to you)

1. You submit your job and any files it needs
2. We run it
3. You get a notification when it's done
4. Download your output — files are kept for 7 days

That's it. There's no step where you provision anything.

---

## What it costs

You buy credits. You spend credits on jobs. No subscription, no seat license, no "contact sales."

When you're done for the month, you stop spending. Nothing renews. Nothing auto-charges.

You pick how fast you need it:

| Speed        | What it means            | Cost           |
| ------------ | ------------------------ | -------------- |
| **Urgent**   | Done in under 15 minutes | More credits   |
| **Standard** | Done within a few hours  | Normal         |
| **Patient**  | Runs whenever it's cheap | Fewest credits |

If you're not in a hurry, Patient mode is cheap. If you need it now, Urgent is there.

---

## Why not just use [other thing]?

**Cloud GPU providers** — you're renting by the hour whether you use it or not. One idle afternoon and you've paid for nothing.

**Running it yourself** — good luck. Hardware is expensive. Setup takes time. Maintenance never stops.

Soggy Roll runs your job, charges you for exactly that job, and gets out of the way.
