**Soggy Roll**

_AI Generation Infrastructure, Made Accessible_

Product Overview & Vision

# **The Problem**

AI generation is powerful — but for most people, it is inaccessible. The tools that exist today either require technical knowledge to set up, are priced for enterprises, or force users into rigid interfaces that don't fit their workflow.

Non-technical users — creators, small business owners, researchers, marketers — are left with three bad options:

- Pay premium prices on consumer platforms with no flexibility

- Struggle with developer-oriented tools they weren't built for

- Give up entirely and miss out on the productivity gains AI offers

The gap isn't in AI capability. It's in accessibility.

# **What We're Building**

Soggy Roll is a managed AI generation platform that gives every user — technical or not — access to powerful GPU-backed generation containers at a fair price.

| The Core Idea Users submit generation requests through a clean, simple interface. Soggy Roll handles everything behind the scenes: resource allocation, model execution, delivery. Results are ready when you need them — no infrastructure knowledge required. |
| :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

# **How It Works**

Soggy Roll abstracts away the complexity of GPU infrastructure into a simple request-and-receive flow.

|   01   | User submits a generation request via the Soggy Roll interface, with optional files and configuration. |
| :----: | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **02** | Soggy Roll queues the job and intelligently allocates it to the best available GPU resource.           |
| **03** | The generation runs in an isolated, managed container — no shared resources, no interference.          |
| **04** | Output is stored securely and the user is notified. Files are available for 7 days.                    |

# **Pricing Philosophy**

Soggy Roll runs on a credit-based system. Users buy credits and spend them on generations. Pricing is transparent, predictable, and tied directly to compute usage — not arbitrary subscription tiers.

| Priority Tiers Urgent — Under 15 mins — Job is dispatched immediately to the best available hardware. Higher credit cost. Standard — Up to 3 hours — Balanced speed and cost. Default for most users. Patient — Up to 6 hours — Job is scheduled during low-demand windows. Cheapest option. |
| :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

This model means casual users pay almost nothing for occasional generations, while power users who need throughput can invest accordingly. Nobody is locked into a monthly bill for a tool they use twice a week.
